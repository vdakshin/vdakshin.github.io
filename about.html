With a background in strategic analytics and data science, Vijay has more recently applied and adapted techniques from these fields to digital marketing, focusing on driving media efficiency and optimizing media spend, with the aim of enabling clients to realize value from their digital investments.

Prior to that he worked for several years in enabling data-driven decision making on enterprise initiatives for telecommunications providers. Within this domain, he has focused on developing time series applications, conducting test and learn experiments and applying machine learning for marketing use cases. He has also uncovered many opportunities to apply advanced analytics and AI to deliver business value.

Vijay holds a MSE in computer engineering and an MA in business administration and currently works as a senior manager of data science at Bell, which is Canada's largest communications company that is leading the way in broadband and media innovation.
